Daily Schedule

8 Days & 7 Nights

Day 1
2020 September

Arrival in Srinagar - The City of Lakes

Arrive at Srinagar Airport, transfer to a houseboat or hotel on Nigeen Lake., enjoy a Shikara ride, visit Mughal Gardens, and relax overnight.

Read more

Day 2
2020 September

Srinagar to Gurez Valley (via Bandipora)

Depart early for Gurez Valley, pass Razdan Pass (11,672 ft), check-in at a homestay in Dawar, and relax overnight.

Read more

Day 3
2020 September

Exploring Gurez Valley

Visit Habba Khatoon Peak, explore Tulail and Sheikhpura villages, enjoy Kishanganga river views, and optionally trek to Khandiyal Top. Stay overnight in Gurez.

Read more

Day 4
2020 September

Gurez to Srinagar

After breakfast, drive to Srinagar, visit Bandipora, enjoy leisure time at Boulevard Road, and shop. Stay overnight in Srinagar.

Read more

Day 5
2020 September

Srinagar to Pahalgam – The Valley of Shepherds

Drive to Pahalgam (2.5 hours), explore Aru Valley, Betaab Valley, and Lidder River, with optional horse riding or nature walks. Stay overnight in Pahalgam.

Read more

Day 6
2020 September

Pahalgam to Gulmarg – The Meadow of Flowers

Drive to Gulmarg (4 hours), check-in, enjoy leisure time, and optionally experience the Gulmarg Gondola. Stay overnight.

Read more

Day 7
2020 September

Gulmarg to Srinagar

Explore Gulmarg in the morning, drive to Srinagar, visit Mughal Gardens, and relax at Shankaracharya Temple or Dal Lake. Stay overnight.

Read more

2020 September

Departure

After breakfast, transfer to Srinagar Airport for departure.
